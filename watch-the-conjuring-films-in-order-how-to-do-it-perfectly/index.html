What to Know

  • The_Conjuring_ movies were not released chronologically.
  • The series timeline spans three decades (1952-1981).
  • Chronologically:The Nun movies,Annabelle: Creation ,Annabelle , and then_The Conjuring_ .

The Conjuring is one of the most prolific horror movie franchises of the last decade, with three main entries and multiple prequels and spinoffs. The movies haven’t come out in chronological order, so this guide will help you keep them straight.

How to Watch the ‘Conjuring’ Movies in Chronological Order

The original 2013 film and its direct sequels are set during the 1970s and into the early 80s. The movies follow the fictional ghost hunting of real-life paranormal investigators Lorraine and Ed Warren.

However, the series has also produced several spinoffs that delve into the backstories of other characters, including the infamous haunted doll Annabelle. This has resulted in a timeline that spans about 30 years (1952 - 1981).

To watch_The Conjuring_ in chronological order of events, follow this viewing guide:

If a movie isn’t available on a subscription streaming platform, you can rent or buy them from platforms like Amazon, Apple TV, and Google Play. You may even get a good deal on a bundle that includes several (or even all) of the movies.

## Will There Be More ‘Conjuring’ Movies?

Plans are currently in the works for a fourth and final_Conjuring_ movie, subtitled_Last Rites_ . It’ll be another story featuring the Warrens, with Patrick Wilson and Vera Farmiga returning. We don’t know yet which case it’s based on, but it’s due out on September 5, 2025.

We’ve also heard that producers Peter Safran and James Wan want to make additional spinoffs and more entries in both the_Annabelle_ and_The Nun_ series, but no details have emerged yet.
