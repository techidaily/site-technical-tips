## Celebrate Pride with These Must-Watch LGBTQ Series and Movies on Netflix - July Edition (202E+t 2024)

Close 

 LGBTQ shows on the[ Netflix streaming service](https://www.lifewire.com/overview-of-the-netflix-streaming-service-1847831) have increased significantly in recent years, with many new series featuring strong gay, bi, lesbian, and trans main characters viewers can relate to and learn from.
