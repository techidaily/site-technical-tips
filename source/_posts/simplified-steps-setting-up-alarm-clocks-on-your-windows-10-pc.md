## Installing Your Epson Printer Made Simple: Follow These Steps

You just bought an Epson printer but don’t know how to install it? No matter which system you’re using, Window or Mac, you can find a step-by-step installation guide here. Read on to find how…

 Before you start the installation, you need to connect your printer to your network so it can be monitored on the computer. Follow the steps in the manual book to set up your printer and connect to your network.

## Install Epson Printer for Windows

1. Turn on the printer.
2. Go to the[Epson official website](https://epson.com/Support/wa00607d) , and choose to download and install the Windows version of Epson Connect Printer Setup Utility.
3. Tick the agreement and click**Next** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/w0.jpg)
4. Click**Install** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/w00.jpg)
5. Select your product in the menu then click**Next** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/w1.jpg)
6. Select**Printer Registration** and click**Next** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/w2.jpg)
7. Click**Agree** \>**Next** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/w3.jpg)
8. Click**OK** when you see**Register a printer to Epson Connect** .
9. You can[create a new account](https://www.epsonconnect.com/guide/en/html/regst%5F1.htm) or sign-up with an existing one.
10. Click**Close** .

 Read[Epson official page](https://epson.com/support/epson-connect-printer-setup-for-windows) for more details.

## Install Epson Printer for Mac

1. Turn on the printer.
2. Go to the[Epson official website](https://epson.com/Support/wa00607d) , and choose to download and install the Mac version of Epson Connect Printer Setup Utility.
3. Click**Continue** .
4. Click**Continue** \>**Agree** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/吗.jpg)
5. Click**Install** \>**Close** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/没.jpg)
6. Select your printer and click**Next** .  
**Note** : If there’s no window pop-up, you can install Epson Printer by open the**Finder** \>**Application** \>**Epson Software** \>**Epson Connect Printer Setup** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/么.jpg)
7. Choose**Printer Registration** then click**Next** \>**OK** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/嘛.jpg)
8. Scroll down the content and tick the**I accept the Terms and Conditions** checkbox, then click**Next** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/买.jpg)
9. You can[create a new account](https://www.epsonconnect.com/guide/en/html/regst%5F1.htm) or sign-up with an existing one.
10. Click**Close** .

 For more details, go to[Epson official page](https://epson.com/support/epson-connect-printer-setup-for-mac-os-x) .
