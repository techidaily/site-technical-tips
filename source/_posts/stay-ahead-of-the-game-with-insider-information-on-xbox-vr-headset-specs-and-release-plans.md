* **Meta Quest 2 release date** : October 13, 2020
* **Meta Quest Pro release date** : October 25, 2022

* **Signature Xbox design** : While not an official product rendering, the image Meta offered in its announcement about Meta Horizon OS shows a black and green VR headset, a hallmark color scheme across Xbox gaming consoles.

##  Xbox VR Headset Specs and Hardware

* 128GB and 512GB versions
* Snapdragon XR2 Gen 2 chip
* 4K+[ Infinite Display](https://www.meta.com/blog/quest/vr-display-optics-pancake-lenses-ppd/)
* 3D spatial audio
* [ Mixed reality](https://www.meta.com/blog/quest/mixed-reality-definition-passthrough-scene-understanding-spatial-anchors/) with RGB cameras.
