## How to Watch X-Men Movies in Order of Release

 By far the most coherent way to watch the X-Men films is in the order of their theatrical and home releases. From there, you can witness how the franchise evolved and changed in style.

_X-Men: Days of Future Past_ is a time travel crossover between the original_X-Men_ trilogy and the_X-Men Beginnings_ prequel series. The 2023 segments of the film take place several years after_The Wolverine_ . For this list, the film counts as an_X-Men Beginnings_ entry.

## How to Stream the X-Men Movies

 After Disney's acquisition of Fox, streaming the_X-Men_ films is a much easier task, as the titles are slowly migrating to[Disney Plus](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-stream-on-disney-4687015) . Otherwise, you can rent all of these movies on digital storefronts such as iTunes, Amazon Prime Video, or[Fandango at Home](https://www.lifewire.com/vudu-adds-tv-shows-on-demand-1847424) .  

 This guide only covers the_X-Men_ and mutant films produced by 20th Century Fox. The franchise is unconnected to the[Marvel Cinematic Universe, which is also available to stream](https://www.lifewire.com/watch-marvel-movies-in-order-online-5077381) , and consists of the original_X-Men_ trilogy, a prequel_X-Men Beginnings_ series, an informal trilogy of_Wolverine_ films, two_Deadpool_ films, and a horror spin-off called_The New Mutant_ s. X-Men TV shows such as_The Gifted_ and_Legion_ are not included.
