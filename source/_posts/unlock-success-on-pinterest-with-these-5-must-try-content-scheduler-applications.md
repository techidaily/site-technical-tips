## Unlock Success on Pinterest with These 5 Must-Try Content Scheduler Applications

Close 

 Whether you run a popular food blog, or if you handle the social media posts for the company you work for,[ Pinterest](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-use-pinterest-3486578) is one social network that can make a big difference in your traffic, sales, opt-ins, and more. 

 Pinterest itself doesn't allow you to schedule pins (Pinterest posts) to be posted at a particular time, but there are many[ third-party tools](https://www.lifewire.com/social-media-apps-for-managing-everything-3486302) that do allow you to schedule pins. A Pinterest scheduler can help you ensure that your pins are being seen by your target audience regularly, and at the right times.

 Here are five great tools to use for scheduling Pinterest posts.

 None of the following tools are free to use indefinitely. If you want to schedule your Pinterest pins past the free trial period, you'll have to pay for one of the following premium social media management tools.

## [ Tailwind](https://www.tailwindapp.com/) 

![Tailwind](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/A4CiMpXYFpel0tLJSA2E2UaEMKk=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ScreenShot2018-04-04at2.45.14PM-5ac51dae642dca0036898f42.jpg) 

 Tailwind is a tool that's specifically focused on Pinterest and[ Instagram](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-use-instagram-3485864) marketing, making it one heck of a great tool for planning and scheduling your visual social media posts.

 Tailwind will suggest times to publish your pins, plus you can create multiple posts all at once from any page on the web, the Tailwind browser extension, or the Tailwind mobile app. Tailwind also allows you to upload images in bulk from almost anywhere and recycle posts that perform well.

 Use the drag-and-drop feature to organize your pins, and take advantage of your automatically optimized queue for super fast and automated pinning.

 What We Like

* Browser extensions for Chrome, Safari, and Firefox.
* Suggests ways to improve your Pinterest account.

 What We Don't Like

* No mobile support
