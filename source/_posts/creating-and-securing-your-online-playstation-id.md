##  How to Create a PSN Account on a PS5

 If you already have a PSN account on your PS4, you can log in using the same account on your PS5 console. If you don't have an account, you can create a new one directly from your PlayStation. Here's how:  

1. Go to the Home screen and select**Add User** .  
![Add User on the PS5 Home screen](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/6tNr9Kti7W32BzcuxYdnGoL158w=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/001_create-a-playstation-network-account-4103872-d17e44953787474584393f85140580aa.jpg)
2. Select**Get Started** .

3. Read the user agreement and then, if you wish to proceed, press the box next to**I Agree** . Select**Confirm** .
4. Select**Create an Account** .  
![Create an Account on PS5 Sign In screen](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/dcotW2YWg9w7tqfOQAYJJHhvj8U=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/003_create-a-playstation-network-account-4103872-18e37a4aac444b03b367ad023aa4f66a.jpg)
5. Over the next few steps, you're asked to fill out a variety of information. This includes your country/region, language preference, and birth date. Follow the on-screen prompts.
6. When asked, enter your email address and create a password for your PSN account. Press**Next** after doing so.
7. The next few steps ask about your name, location, and avatar. Complete the on-screen questions, pressing**Next** after each one.  
 The name you pick is seen when you first power on your PlayStation.  
![A list of avatars for a new PSN account on a PS5](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/fSAU6ly4H4IESU8_Y-ibWx-TCEk=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/psn-avatar-ps5-5e685e93db164b05904cfc7b99cf6cee.png)
8. Type the Online ID you want to use, then press**Next** .  
 Don't use your real name! This ID is seen by other people in games, messages, and other areas.
9. On the privacy settings screen, select one of the available profiles. Or, choose**Customize Settings** to define things like who can see your real name and profile image in search results, within games, etc.
10. On the Data Collection screen, you're asked if Sony can use your usage data to improve its services. It's your choice: select**Limited Data Only** or**Confirm and Continue** .
11. When asked about personalized ads and purchase recommendations, pick either**Disable and Continue** or**Enable and Continue** .
12. Check**I Agree** on the Important Legal Documents screen, then select**Confirm** .
13. Check your email! Select the**Verify Now** link in the email from Sony to confirm your account.
14. On the Secure Your Account screen on your PS5, select whether you want to require a passcode to log in and whether your password should be required at checkout. Select**OK** .
15. Select**Text Message** or**Authenticator App** if you want to turn on two-factor authentication. Or, select**Do This Later** .
16. Optionally add a phone number to your account that Sony can use to help you reset your account if you can't access it for whatever reason. Choose**Do This Later** to skip this step.
17. Finally, select**OK** . You've made a PlayStation Network account!

 FAQ

* How do I delete my PSN account?  
[ Deleting your PSN account](https://www.playstation.com/en-us/support/account/close-account-for-psn/) is an involved process.[ Contact Sony directly](https://www.playstation.com/en-us/support/account/close-account-for-psn/) and request to close it, while providing your account ID and the email address associated with it. Once closed, you won't be able to reuse that PSN account name and you will lose access to all of its associated purchases, subscriptions, and leftover funds in your wallet.
* How do I change the email address on my PSN account?  
 Computer: Visit[ Account Management](https://www.playstation.com/acct/management) \>**Security** \>**Edit** next to "Sign-In ID," set new email,**Save** . PS5:**Settings** \>**Users and Accounts** \>**Account** \>**Sign-in ID (email address)** , enter new email,**Save** . PS4:**Settings** \>**Account Management** \>**Account Information** \>**Sign-in ID** , enter password, set new email >**Confirm** .
