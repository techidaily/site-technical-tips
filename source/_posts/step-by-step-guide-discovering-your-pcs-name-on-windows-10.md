##  Using File Explorer

 You can also use Windows File Explorer to find your computer name.

 While you're [in a File Explorer window](https://youtube-web.techidaily.com/ed-2024-approved-streamers-financial-health-check-in-youtubers/) (press Windows+E to open it if it isn't already), in the left sidebar, [right-click](https://desktop-recording.techidaily.com/updated-the-art-of-recording-fun-6-techniques-to-document-minecraft-for-2024/) "This PC" and choose "Properties."

![Right-click "This PC" and select "Properties."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/6-this-pc-properties.png) 

 You'll open an "About" page. Here, next to "Device Name," your PC name is displayed.
