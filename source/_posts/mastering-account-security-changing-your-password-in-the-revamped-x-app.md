###  What to Know

* On Twitter.com, go to**More** \>**Settings and Privacy** \>**Change your password** .
* On the[ X (formerly Twitter) app](https://www.lifewire.com/best-mobile-twitter-apps-3486098) , go to**Settings and privacy** \>**Account** \>**Password** .
* To reset a forgotten password, select**Forgot password?** on the sign-in page.

 You should update your X (formerly Twitter) credentials often to keep your account secure and safe from hackers. Here are several ways to change an X password.

##  Change X Password From the X App 

 Changing your X password from the X mobile app is similar to changing it on the X website.

1. Open the X app on your smart device.
2. Tap your profile picture.
3. Tap**Settings and privacy** near the bottom of the screen.
4. Tap**Account** .  
![Twitter - accessing Account settings](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/5bTdvzNzI1NCT1Bt5LL_TWuF5Ng=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/1ann-989dc6d46d83491d8605fca65fdd639f.jpg)
5. Under the**Login and security** heading, tap**Password** .
6. Enter your current password in the**Current password** text box.
7. Enter the new password you want to use in the**New password** text box.
8. Enter the new password a second time in the**Confirm password** text box.  
![Twitter- change password](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/B2G-c4QAiyUWFnMbYF_qb8UmWk8=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/2ann-41ee8178389f432dae2b75aeac5b1552.jpg)
9. Tap**Update Password** .

##  Change X Password From the X Mobile Website 

 When you want to change your X password using a web browser on your Android or iPhone, use the X mobile site. The steps to change your X password using the X mobile website are different than using the X website.

1. Open your favorite mobile web browser.
2. Go to the X mobile website.
3. Sign in using your current password.
4. Tap your**profile image** in the upper-left corner.
5. Tap**Settings and privacy** .
6. Tap**Your account** .  
![Twitter - change password on mobile website](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/YKCvyAUiEUQ7PpYzOvOxfKm_xfE=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann2-d580b7de214840bca441b8bb343bbeb4.jpg)
7. Tap**Change your password** .
8. Enter your current password in the**Current password** text box.
9. Enter the new password you want to use in the**New password** text box.
10. Enter the new password a second time in the**Confirm password** text box.  
![Twitter - change password on mobile website](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/TRBppuNzoB4jUdFBnglYewInWyU=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann3-265c2f27f71f448f85d89092a5ed2162.jpg)

##  Reset an X Password You've Lost or Forgotten 

 Passwords get lost and forgotten. When you can’t remember your X password, reset it.

 X requires an email address or a phone number to reset a lost or forgotten password. Make sure you’ve added one or both to your account.

1. Go to the X sign-in page.
2. Select**Forgot password?**  
![Twitter - forgot password](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/3bE_c9uJ65rKOiacJdoI_8Nb0ho=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann1forgotpassword-cb611a9a41df489f8e1c6db5f254cfc1.jpg)
3. Enter your email address, phone number, or X username in the text box.  
![Twitter - find account via email, phone or username](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/Ww22y0sloevYpkSmkCinWcuUAfA=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann2enteremailaddresetc-3ddd1e5b5ddf43f2962b2453efdb8b28.jpg)
4. Select**Search** . You’ll be asked to choose how to reset your password.
