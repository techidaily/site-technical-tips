###  What to Know

* Go to X (formerly Twitter) and sign up. Enter info > verify account > set up profile > add interests > choose accounts to follow.
* Change name: Select**profile** icon > tap**Settings and Privacy** \> enter_name_ \>**Save** .

 This article explains how to set up and get started with a new X (formerly Twitter) account on a web browser. Creating an account with the mobile app is almost identical, so the same steps apply.

##  How to Join X via the Website

 Signing up for X is a lot like other websites, so it's a fairly stress-free process.

1. Go to[ https://twitter.com](https://twitter.com)
2. Select the sign-up option you want to use. You can use your phone number/email or your Google account.  
 Mac, iPhone, and iPad users have an extra option of using their Apple ID.  
![The Twitter sign-up page](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/cbCmbncz5bxfOBMEQYMvKb5svuQ=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/TwitterSign-up-78b263d2da054b3cb0b3ed296c654a49.jpg)  
Twitter
3. Enter your name and phone number.  
![Twitter Create Your Account Screen](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/Jx7u3Fzp85pFzOo8myzdeJlIZWU=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/how-to-set-up-a-twitter-account-45898571-88a078dcffbf4e10ace0a0a247e82e69.jpg)  
 Alternatively, to enter your email address, select**Use Email Instead** .
4. Select**Next** .
5. Choose whether you want people to be able to find you via your phone number or email address, whether you want to receive emails about X, and if you want to receive personalized ads as you use X.  
![Twitter Customize Your Experience Screen](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/-FXPD3MJ6nA8fqCgYFZfPpWRVt8=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/how-to-set-up-a-twitter-account-45898572-7630802b29524f8b8e7511e551b2840c.jpg)  
 You don't have to opt into any of these if you don't want to. It's entirely optional.
6. Select**Next** .
7. Ensure you've entered your details correctly, then select**Sign Up** .
8. Wait for a verification code to be sent to the phone number or email address you entered.  
![Twitter verification code screen](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/pD9O4CyV7yciCicBtEi0GSq92rA=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/how-to-set-up-a-twitter-account-45898573copy-092be3dde5fb45d1b73ed7fc1bb166e4.jpg)
9. Enter the verification code.  
 The codes expire after two hours. Check your email or messages and complete this step before then.
10. Select**Next** .
11. Enter a password, then select**Next** .  
![Twitter new password screen](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/ufrIIUIhTc4hrPOUlUquqiDB5_E=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/how-to-set-up-a-twitter-account-45898574-09f99a84349a484fb61bc6a80270e47e.jpg)  
 Make sure your password is a[ secure one](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-create-a-strong-password-4783955) that's hard to guess.
12. Choose a profile picture, then select**Next** .  
![Twitter pick a profile picture screen](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/eM25Bx4zWOHZyAXnJUUlk6D2_Fs=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/how-to-set-up-a-twitter-account-45898576-d91a1b56137f43748e15c9f7677ce3a9.jpg)  
 Choose a profile picture that matches your personality or general goals for your X account, whether that means your business logo or a fun photo of your pets.

##  What to Do After Joining X

 Joining X is simple enough, but you're not finished. Here are some steps to make your experience more personalized.

### Change Your X Username

 X assigns you a random username based on the name you enter when signing up. Here's how to change it.

1. Select the silhouette in the upper-right corner, then choose**Settings and Privacy** .
2. In the Username field at the top of the screen, enter what you'd like your new username to be, then scroll down and select**Save Changes** .  
![Twitter username change screen](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/2Sp8486ND4vgatD_lImEnruL--s=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/how-to-set-up-a-twitter-account-458985712copy-7fd43d1d9d804bcaa77aeeecb5e5e317.jpg)  
 As you type your new username, X tells you if it's available. Be original with your choice.
3. You're done! You can now find your profile at https://twitter.com/yourusernamehere.

 There are thousands of people on X, from friends to celebrities, politicians, sportspeople, and local businesses. Use X's search bar in the upper-right corner of the home page to search for people who interest you.

### Join In!

 Conversation and interaction are key to getting the most from the service. Get to know people. Talk to them by tweeting with them. Don't be offensive or rude, but get to know people and join in.
