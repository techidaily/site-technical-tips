##  How to Use Facebook Image Search 

 Facebook assigns a numerical ID to all photos uploaded to the social media channel. Images downloaded from Facebook have that numerical ID as part of the file name by default. If you know this number, you can use it to find the source of the picture on Facebook.

 Using this ID number might help you locate the profile picture of the person who shared it, or the person whose profile you are trying to find might be named or tagged in the image.

1. Right-click the image you want to search for on Facebook.
2. Select**Open in New Tab** in Google Chrome.  
 If you are using a different web browser, select**View Photo** ,**View Image** , or a similar option.

3. Look for three sets of numbers separated by underscores in the address bar or file name of the image, such as those highlighted in this example.

##  Reverse Image Search: Facebook and Google Method 

 You can perform a reverse image search in Google using a photo posted on Facebook to possibly learn more about who posted it.

1. Right-click the image and select**Search image with Google** .  
![Search Image with Google option selected on an image in the Chrome web browser.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/kLEcAjzfDPOXZqoWdYgjFKNfCRA=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/005_use-facebook-image-search-4590290-63a3338a33a34d7f939ca328499a5ee9.jpg)
2. Select**Find Image Source** from the[ Google Lens](https://www.lifewire.com/google-lens-4153383) side panel displaying possible matches for the photo.  
![Google Lens Find image source options from Google Chrome.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/dzHdftIyh0zB9tA7XxFXiENT4aI=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/006_use-facebook-image-search-4590290-765aa9752ebc4c73990959b711974d83.jpg)  
 Alternatively, you can search for an image you have downloaded by uploading it or dragging and dropping it onto the Google Images search page to use the[ Google Reverse Image](https://www.lifewire.com/use-google-reverse-image-search-4582422) search.
3. Check the list for Facebook profiles linked to the image.

 FAQ

* How do I clear my Facebook search history?  
 To[ clear your Facebook search history](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-clear-your-facebook-search-history-4798829) , select the search bar, then choose**Edit** \>**Clear Searches** . You can also remove individual searches from**Accounts Center** \>**Your information and permissions** .
* How do I search Facebook posts?  
 To[ search for Facebook posts](https://www.lifewire.com/facebook-advanced-search-tips-2653972) , enter a word or group of words into the search bar and select**Posts** . Select**Posts From** to filter results by posts from your friends, your groups and pages, or public posts.
* How do I block searches for my Facebook profile?  
 To[ block searches for your Facebook profile](https://www.lifewire.com/block-searches-on-facebook-3482915) , go to your profile and select**Settings & Privacy** \>**Settings** . Find the**How people find and contact You** section (below**Audience and Visibility** ) to customize your profile search settings.
